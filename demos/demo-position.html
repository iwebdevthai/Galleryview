GalleryView » Element Positioning

This demo illustrates how both the filmstrip and informational overlays can be moved to suit the needs of the user. The filmstrip can be placed on any of the gallery's four sides. Also note that for vertical filmstrips, the navigation buttons will arrange themselves vertically, depending on the width of the filmstrip frames. The overlays can be placed on the bottom or top of the panel. This demo also shows off another panel animation style, crossfading between images.

$('#myGallery').galleryView({
    filmstrip_position: 'right',
    enable_overlays: true,
    overlay_position: 'top',
    panel_animation: 'crossfade'
});
